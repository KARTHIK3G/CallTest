Voice Communication Setup

Follow the steps below to establish a voice call with another user:

Step 1: Create and Share Offer

Click the "Create Offer" button to generate an offer. Copy the offer details and share it with your partner.

Step 2: Enter Partner's Offer/Answer

If you received an offer from your partner, paste it below and click "Set Remote Description" to respond. Alternatively, if you already created the offer, paste the answer you received from your partner here.

Step 3: Exchange ICE Candidates

Share your ICE candidate with your partner and paste theirs below to finalize the connection.

Step 4: Start or End Call

Once the above steps are complete, you can start the call. Click "End Call" when you are done.
